Step-by-Step Activation Process

Activating Hulu is simple. Follow these steps to link your device with your Hulu account:

  1. Open the Hulu app on your smart TV or streaming device.
  2. Sign in with your Hulu credentials or create a new account.
  3. Note the activation code displayed on your screen.
  4. Visit Hulu.com/activate on your computer or mobile browser.
  5. Enter the activation code and click “Activate.”
  6. Wait for confirmation — your device will refresh automatically.

Fix Hulu.com/activate Errors

If you face issues during activation, try these quick fixes:

  • Clear browser cache and cookies.
  • Ensure a stable internet connection.
  • Update your Hulu app or browser.
  • Restart your device and retry activation.
  • Generate a new activation code if the old one expires.
